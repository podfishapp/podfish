What is Personal Data?

"Personal data" refers to any information that can be used to identify an individual, either directly or indirectly. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Device identifiers (such as unique device ID, IP address)
  • Usage data and analytics information
  • Contact information (name, email address) when provided voluntarily
  • Any other information that could reasonably be used to identify you

Under the GDPR, personal data means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

The Service uses the built-in speech recognizer functionality of iOS and iPadOS provided by Apple. For recognizing your speech, your voice data is sent to Apple's servers and may be cached locally on your device. The voice data is not sent to our servers or any other third party. The transcription the app receives from Apple's servers is then translated using Google's AI services. To provide the translation, the text is transmitted to Google for processing. Depending on the specific Google service used, Google may act either as our processor (Firebase/Vertex AI under data processing terms) or as an independent controller under its own terms. We configure available settings to disable training/logging where possible; we do not store translations on our own servers.

AI Translation (Firebase AI / Google Gemini)

When you use translation features, the app sends the text to be translated ("strings") to Google services (Firebase AI / Google Gemini) for processing. Depending on your location, this processing may occur on servers in the United States. We do not store these strings on our own servers; they are transmitted to Google solely to provide the translation and returned to your device.

Please avoid including sensitive personal data in text you submit for translation. Processing by Google is governed by Google's terms and privacy notices. For details, see Firebase Privacy and Security and the Google Privacy Policy. We configure available settings to prevent use of submitted content for model training and disable logging where possible.

Legal basis (GDPR): Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) to provide translations you request; and our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) in operating and securing our services.
