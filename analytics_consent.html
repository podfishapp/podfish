Analytics & Optimization

What data do we collect?

If you consent to analytics, we collect anonymized usage data to improve the app experience:

  • App usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • General geographic region (country level)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services for analytics:

Firebase Analytics (Google)
Used to understand how users interact with the app.
Firebase Analytics Privacy

Firebase Crashlytics (Google)
Used to detect and fix app crashes.
Firebase Privacy Policy

AppsFlyer
Used for marketing attribution and campaign measurement.
AppsFlyer Privacy Policy

Your Choice

This consent is optional. You can use the app without enabling analytics. You can change your preference at any time in the app settings.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months by Firebase and in accordance with each service provider's data retention policies.

Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing this data is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).
